Privacy Policy

We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

Information We Collect

  • Personal details (name, email, phone number, location)
  • Profile information submitted by househelps and employers
  • Usage data from website interactions

How We Use Your Information

  • To connect househelps with employers
  • To improve our services and user experience
  • To communicate with users about updates or inquiries

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, please note that no system is completely secure. Users are advised to share personal information carefully when contacting matches directly.

Important Notice

HouseHelpKonnect helps connect househelps and employers. We do not employ househelps directly and are not responsible for agreements made outside our platform. Users should meet and finalize terms personally.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our privacy practices, please contact us via the Contact Us page.

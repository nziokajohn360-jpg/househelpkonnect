How HouseHelpKonnect Works

1. Fill a Simple Form

Househelps fill in their details and the type of work they are looking for. Employers submit the kind of help they need.

2. We Match You

We match both sides based on location, skills, and availability to ensure compatibility.

3. You Connect Directly

Once matched, you can contact each other directly to finalize arrangements and agreements.

Important Notice:

HouseHelpKonnect helps connect househelps and employers. We do not employ househelps directly and do not take responsibility for agreements made outside the platform. Users are advised to meet and agree on terms personally.
